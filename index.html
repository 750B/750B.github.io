SOUTH BRUNSWICK HIGH SCHOOL ROBOTICS

Welcome

We are South Brunswick High School's Robotics Team located in South Brunswick New Jersey. We are a student-run organization that currently participates in the VRC VEX Robotics Competition.Throughout our 14 years of history, we have experienced many successes that have made us one of the most prestigious robotics clubs in Washington State. Our club utilizes a senior-freshmen student mentorship program to help educate freshmen teams so they can create an effective robot. Additionally, we also host the Exothermic Winter Bash every year to help bring STEM and robotics to our community. We are delighted to have 8 teams this year!
